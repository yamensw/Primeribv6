About Prime Rig

Prime Rig is a curated set of browser-based AR experiences that help restaurants showcase menus, brands present products and art in a dust-free virtual showroom, and real estate teams communicate space with confidence.

Origin

AR has been around for nearly a decade in social platforms like Snapchat and Meta experiences, and it’s been adopted by top retailers and consumer tech brands. Prime Rig focuses on the missing piece: bringing that same interaction into practical business applications—menus, product pages, sales enablement, and real-estate visualization—without requiring an app install.

Origin Story

How Prime Rig came to be

AR has been around for nearly a decade in social platforms like Snapchat and Meta experiences, and it’s been adopted by top retailers and consumer tech brands. Prime Rig focuses on the missing piece: bringing that same interaction into practical business applications—menus, product pages, sales enablement, and real-estate visualization—without requiring an app install.

— Founder

Prime Rig started from a simple problem: businesses had access to incredible AR capabilities, but no practical way to deploy them fast, consistently, and beautifully. We built Prime Rig to productize WebAR—using proven platforms and best practices—so companies can launch showrooms that feel premium, load quickly, and work across modern devices.
